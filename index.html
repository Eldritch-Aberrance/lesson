Stygian Site

  • Xsaiav'lairnn
    • Also known as That Which Is Not, they are a strange eldritch being obsessed with The Unknown.
  • Maze
    • A self-appointed body guard of That Which Is Not, Maze is a quiet, unreadable, but very loyal assassin.
  • ENTITY
    • Once one of "The Entities" in That Which Is Not's realm, they've aquired a new form they aren't quite sure what to do with.
  • Ø
    • Another being which seems confounded at existing, "Null" has a strong connection to the concept of Nothing, and their presence was only revealed when That Which Is Not's kingdom expanded to absorb Null's domain.
  • Eslinthe
    • An excitable cheery being, she is eager to make friends and do her best. Seeks to be That Which Is Not's student in the arcane arts.
  • Feravinh
    • A merchant and trader of strange magical and mysterious artifacts, they are often out in dangerous places to procure and sell most interesting wares. Little about their personal life beyond their business is known.
  • Amsajin
    • A strange entiy who's motives are unclear. She seems to have a connection to spirits and necromancy, but often 'plays the role' of an outlandish, dashing rogue. Has an odd fondness for Maze.
  • Vriahs
    • An aggressive being that seems to only enjoy filling things with holes. Their insistence seems more like a madness, as though something has been broken within them...
